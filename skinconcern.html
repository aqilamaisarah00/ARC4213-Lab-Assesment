Skin Concern

From dullness to rough texture to sensitive skin, here are 5 of the most common skin concerns and what you can do to address them.

Common Skin Concern

Our primary focus is on addressing skincare concerns that are particularly relevant to individuals living in Malaysia. Given the unique environmental conditions, such as the tropical climate and high humidity levels, Malaysian skin faces specific challenges that may differ from those experienced in other regions.

Acne/Blemishes Skin Concern

Acne is a common skin condition that happens when hair follicles under the skin become clogged. Sebum oil that helps keep skin from drying out and dead skin cells plug the pores, which leads to outbreaks of lesions, commonly called pimples or zits

    There are several types of lesions, or pimples. It included :

  • Whiteheads : Plugged hair follicles that produce white bumps
  • Blackheads : Plugged follicles that reach the surface and open up
    The look black on the skin surface because of the air discolors the sebum.
  • Papules : Inflamed lesions that appear as small, pink bumps and can be
    tender to touch.
  • Pustules : Papules topped by white or yellow pus-filled lesions that may be red
    at the base.
  • Nodules : Large, painful solid lesions that are lodged deep within the skin

Hyperpigmentation

Uneven skin pigmentation (or hyperpigmentation as it is often known) is a common skin complaint. Dark spots known as age spots or sun spots or dark patches of skin frequently appear on the face, hands and other parts of the body regularly exposed to the sun. Hyperpigmentation appears as darkened patches or spts on the skin that make skin look uneven. The spots are known as age spots or sun spots.

Tiny Bumps

Forehead acne can appear as tiny bumps. It may be caused by Comedones. Comedonal acne happens when dead skin cells and oil or sebum, block your pores and form bumps on your skin. It is not a serious condition, but if you don't like how it looks, here are some of the ingredients that can help with tiny bumps.

  • Benzoyl Peroxide : Used to treat mild to moderate acne. Works by reducing
    the amount of acne-causing bacteria
  • Salicylic Acid : Treat acne by reducing swelling and redness and unplugging
    blocked skin pores to allow pimple to shrink
  • Retinol : Increases skin cell production, unclog pores, exfoliates your skin and
    increases collagen production.

Sensitive Skin

It's a common condition which means your skin is more prone to reactions such as redness and itching. Most people who have sensitive skin notice occasional or frequent itching, burning and stinging of patches of skin.

Dull Skin

Dull Skin is skin that looks laclustre, flat, or even grey, and may even feel rough or bumpy to touch. What causes dull skin? It's the result of dead skin cell build-up on the surface of your skin, and it can happen at any age and in any weather.
